EduFrame is a focused lesson-plan notebook for teachers.

Plan tonight. Teach tomorrow.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Write structured lesson plans (objectives, materials, activities, homework, notes)
• Organize classes and sections you teach
• Build a weekly timetable with reminders before each period
• Browse and search every plan in your notebook
• Duplicate plans to another date
• Export and share PDFs for your HOD or co-teachers
• Get AI Assist for activities, homework, differentiation, and plan improvements
• Work offline — your plans live on your device
• Back up to Google Drive or a shared file, and restore when you need to
• Use Hindi labels and dark mode

PRIVACY & YOUR DATA
Lesson plans stay on your device unless you export, share, or back them up. Sign-in uses Google. AI Assist sends only the prompts you choose to our secure proxy (Groq). You can delete all local EduFrame data from Settings.
